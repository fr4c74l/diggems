About the game

Dig Gems is a board game where two players compete, in turns, for the location of more precious gems in a mine. The game is fast, during few seconds per section and demands – besides a bit of luck – logical thinking and strategy for both players.

Dig Gems is a Minesweeper Flags clone for web, made widespread by the now extinct MSN Messenger, which, was based and has lots of similarities with the classic Minesweeper puzzle, made famous by Microsoft Windows's distribution version.

The main difference between Minesweeper and Dig Gems is that in Minesweeper, your objective is to avoid special positions – the land mines – and open all the remaining terrain to win. In Dig Gems, the objective is exactly the opposite: you must find the special positions – precious gems – to win points and overcome your adversary.

Dig Gems doesn't require installation or some special plugin to be played instead, we used the latest technologies in its development, commonly known as HTML5. So, you have to use a relatively new browser to play, that supports all resources used. Therefore, we recommend Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome, both free and open source.

How to play

Hello miner! I suppose you ought to be the latest Burma autarch. So pick up your shovel and pickaxe and get to work! Shown below is the mine, your goal is to dig it and find as much gems as you can before you adversary does, after all, you don't want to be enslaved by it, right!? Gems are rare, they say there are only 51 of them burried in the mine, so stop wasting time get right now to digging!




To open up your mine, you just need to send the link found right bellow the mine to your adversary. As soon as it connects, the run for the gems starts. But wait!!! You don't have a shovel! We can lend you one, but take great care of it, ok? It is the only one we have, and other miners borrows it, too, that is why all the action takes place in turns. So, wait for your shovel or pickaxe to show up, allowing you to cast your move.




You have tools to help you in your quest. Besides your brain and your shovel, you have a gem proximity detector. When you dig, a number is shown. It means exactly how many gems are in immediate proximity of the dug tile. To be more precise, in the 8 positions around the central one.

For instance, in the rightmost picture, the number 1 highligted says there are precisely one gem hidden around that position. Since 7 of those positions are already dug and revealed, there is solely one place where the gem could be. You would just need to click (dig) that tile to find-it.

This way, the numbers shown as the mine is dug are the clues telling the number of gems in the vicinity. When there are no gems about, you can dig like crazy mole until the numbers are found, revealing all the empty space around.




Caso você esteja perdendo o jogo, ou seja, a quando a quantidade de pedras preciosas que você encontrou ― mostrada no topo da mina ― é inferior a do seu oponente, você pode usar uma única carga de dinamite para explodir parte da mina. A potente dinamite abre um vão de 5 por 5 posições! As figuras abaixo mostram a dinamite sendo utilizada pelo minerador vermelho.




A escavação se encerra quando um dos mineradores encontra mais da metade das pedras preciosas. Ao final, é relevada a posição de todas as pedras preciosas que não foram encontradas ― contabilizadas ao vencedor!

Boa cavucada!

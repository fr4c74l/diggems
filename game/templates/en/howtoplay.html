How to play

Howdy miner! I suppose you ought to be the latest Burma ruler. So pick up your shovel and pickaxe and get to work! Shown below is the mine, your goal is to dig it and find as much gems as you can before you adversary does, after all, you don't want to be enslaved by him/her, right!? Gems are rare, they say there are only 51 of them buried in the mine, so stop wasting time get right now to digging!




To open up your mine, you just need to send the link found right bellow the mine to your adversary. As soon as her/him connects, the run for the gems starts. But wait!!! You don't have a shovel! We can lend you one, but take great care of it, OK? It is the only one we have, and other miners borrows it, too, that is why all the action takes place in turns. So, wait for your shovel or pickaxe to show up, allowing you to cast your move. Use it right away! You have only 45 seconds before risking having the shovel taken from you... or worse! You can even be expelled from the mine, at the mercy of your adversary!




You have tools to help you in your quest. Besides your brain and your shovel, you have a gem proximity detector. When you dig, a number is shown. It means exactly how many gems are in immediate proximity of the dug tile. More precisely, in the 8 positions around the central one.

For instance, in the rightmost picture, the number 1 highlighted says there are precisely one gem hidden around that position. Since 7 of those positions are already dug and revealed, there is solely one place where the gem could be. You would just need to click (dig) that tile to find-it.

This way, the numbers shown as the mine is dug are the clues telling the number of gems in the vicinity. When there are no gems about, you will dig like a crazy mole until the numbers are found, revealing all the empty space around.




In case you are losing the game, i.e. the amount of gems you've found―shown in the top of the mine―is smaller than your adversary's, you can use your single explosive charge to blow up part of the mine. The powerful dynamite opens up hole of 5x5 tiles! In the following pictures you can see the charge being used by the red miner.




The mining is over when one of the diggers finds more than half of all mine's gems. After that, the location of all undiscovered gems is revealed―accounted to the winner!

Good digging!
